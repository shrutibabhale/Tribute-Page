LATA MANGESHKAR


28 SEPTEMBER 1929 - 6 FEBRUARY 2022


HISTORY:
Lata Mangeshkar was born in Indore, Madhya Pradesh, India, on September 28, 1929. Among the five kids of her parents, Lata was the oldest. Her parents' names are Pandit Deenanath Mangeshkar and Shevanti Mangeshkar, respectively. Her father, Master Dinanath, was well-liked and admired Marathi's theatrical character.
Lata Mangeshkar was named "Hema" after she was born. In honor of a female character called Latika, her parents changed her name to Lata under one of her father's plays, Bhaaw Bandhan. Asha, Meena, Usha, and Hridaynath are her siblings' names. They're all talented vocalists and guitarists.
Her academic background isn't well-known, but she demonstrated that a certificate isn't the only way to earn a respectful living. Her father was the one who gave her initial music training. She began working as an actor in her father's musical productions when she was five.
